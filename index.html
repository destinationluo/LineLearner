Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2025

Introduction

LineFlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how LineFlow handles your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

LineFlow stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Script Files: PDF documents you import into the app
  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you create during rehearsal sessions
  • Annotations: Notes, highlights, and tags you add to scripts
  • User Preferences: App settings including language selection, appearance preferences, and rehearsal configurations
  • Script Metadata: Role selections, character information, and rehearsal progress

Automatically Collected Information

LineFlow does not automatically collect any information about your usage, device, or behavior.

How We Use Your Information

All information collected by LineFlow is stored locally on your device and is used solely to:

  • Display and manage your imported scripts
  • Enable script reading and annotation features
  • Facilitate rehearsal with audio playback and recording
  • Persist your preferences and settings
  • Provide role highlighting and character tracking

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All your data remains on your device. LineFlow does not transmit any data to external servers or cloud services.
  • No Account Required: LineFlow does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.
  • Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

Permissions

LineFlow requests the following permissions:

Microphone Access

  • Purpose: To record audio for rehearsal purposes (recording scene partner lines)
  • Usage: Audio recordings are stored locally on your device and are only created when you actively initiate recording
  • Control: You can deny microphone access, but rehearsal recording features will not be available

Data Sharing and Third Parties

LineFlow does not:

  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell your personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Transmit data to external servers

Data Retention and Deletion

  • All data remains on your device until you manually delete it
  • Deleting a script removes all associated annotations, recordings, and metadata
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device

Children's Privacy

LineFlow does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

International Users

LineFlow is available worldwide. All data processing occurs locally on your device, regardless of your location.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your scripts, recordings, and annotations within the app
  • Modification: Edit or update any information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual scripts, recordings, or annotations, or uninstall the app to remove all data
